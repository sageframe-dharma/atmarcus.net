**Slider** — Horizontal scroll with snap. Three cards sit side by side on desktop. On narrower screens they scroll left/right with momentum. Thin scrollbar appears on hover. To add a new card when you publish, you just duplicate this block inside the `writing-cards` div:

```html
<a class="writing-card" href="https://sageframe.substack.com/p/YOUR-SLUG" target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <div class="writing-card-img" style="background-image: url('img/YOUR-IMAGE.jpg')"></div>
  <div class="writing-card-body">
    <div class="writing-card-title">Your Title</div>
    <div class="writing-card-desc">Your one-line description.</div>
  </div>
</a>
```

Drop the image in `public/img/`, add the card, deploy. The slider handles any number of cards — four, five, ten, doesn't matter. They just keep scrolling.

**680 × 360px**

That's 340 (max card width) × 180 (image height) at 2x for retina. Crop all three to exactly that and they'll be pixel-perfect with no wasted bytes.
